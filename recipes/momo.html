Mo:MO

Description:

Momo is a popular Nepali and Tibetan steamed dumpling filled with minced meat or vegetables, served with spicy dipping sauce.

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Water & salt (for dough)
  • Minced chicken, buff, or vegetables
  • Onion, garlic, ginger
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, pepper)
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Make a smooth dough with flour, water, and salt.
  2. Prepare filling by mixing minced meat/vegetables with onion, garlic, ginger, spices, and oil.
  3. Roll dough into small circles and fill with mixture.
  4. Shape into dumplings and steam for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with momo achar (chutney).
