Tea

Description:

Tea is a widely consumed hot beverage made by brewing tea leaves in hot water, often flavored with milk, sugar, or spices.

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken, mutton, or vegetables
  • Onion, tomato, ginger, garlic
  • Yogurt
  • Whole spices (cardamom, cinnamon, bay leaf, cloves)
  • Ground spices (turmeric, chili, garam masala)
  • Oil or ghee
  • Coriander & mint leaves

Steps:

  1. Wash and soak rice; cook halfway and set aside.
  2. Fry onions, ginger, garlic, and tomatoes with spices to make gravy.
  3. Add marinated meat/vegetables and cook until tender.
  4. Layer cooked rice over meat mixture, garnish with fried onions, coriander, and mint.
  5. Cover tightly and cook on low heat (dum) for 20â€“25 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with raita or salad.
